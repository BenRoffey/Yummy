# Yummy
A Balatro mod that adds more food jokers, and food-themed consumables, enhancements, seal, booster packs and a special Boss Blind!

Requires Steamodded - Download here https://github.com/Steamodded/smods/wiki

Simply download Yummy and drop into your Mods folder

## Jokers
Chef - Creates a random food joker on round start

Chocolate - X6 Mult, loses X1 Mult per hand played

Pasta - X1 Mult, gains X1 Mult per hand played. Removed at the end of the round

Porridge - X1 Chips for each food joker

Curry - X2 Mult, loses X0.01 Mult per card played

Pizza - +12 Mult per owned Consumeable card

Burrito - Each card held in hand gives X1.5 Mult, reduced by X0.1 Mult each round

Ego - Gains $1 for each food joker eaten

Soup - Each played card permanently gains +1 Mult when scored (Removed after 25 cards)

Coffee - Doubles base hand Mult. -$3 at the end of the round

## Enhancements
Preheating - +15 Chips. Becomes Underdone after being scored

Underdone - +3 Mult. Becomes Cooked after being scored

Cooked - X1.5 Mult. 1 in 6 chance to be destroyed after being scored

## Tarot
Baking Tray - Enhances 3 selected cards to Preheating

## Spectral
Service - Add a Yummy Seal to 1 selected card in your hand

## Boosters
Appetizer Pack - Choose 1 of up to 2 Food Joker cards

A la Carte Pack - Choose 1 of up to 4 Food Joker cards

2 Course Deal Pack - Choose 2 of up to 4 Food Joker cards

## Boss Blind
Gordon - Insults you

## Seal
Yummy Seal - +10 Mult when held in hand. Then, destroyed
